Zac currently serves as a Staff AI Software Engineer at [**Modular**](https://www.modular.com/), focusing on 
developing innovative AI software and high-performance machine learning infrastructure. Prior to Modular, 
Zac was an integral part of Google's pioneering [**Fuchsia OS**](https://fuchsia.dev/) project, where he played a key role 
in developing system libraries, drivers, toolchains, and a new Bluetooth stack. He was instrumental in enabling Apple's
Swift programming language to run natively on Fuchsia ([The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/2017/11/20/16679342/google-fuchsia-os-swift-language-support)).

Zac previously held senior engineering positions at notable technology
firms, including [**Apportable**](https://venturebeat.com/2016/08/18/google-secretly-acqui-hired-mobile-app-development-startup-apportable/),
[**SeatMe (acquired by Yelp)**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/zbowling), [**doubleTwist**](https://www.doubletwist.com/),
[**Texas Instruments**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/zbowling), and [**Match.com**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/zbowling).
At Apportable, Zac was crucial in developing an SDK that allowed developers to port native Objective-C and Swift applications to
Android, Linux, and other platforms, significantly influencing mobile application development.

His professional specializations include:

- Operating systems and compiler development
- Embedded systems and mobile platforms
- Rust, Swift, C++, Dart, and Go development
- Developer evangelism and community engagement
