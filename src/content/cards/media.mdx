Zac's innovative work and outspoken advocacy have attracted extensive media coverage. He has been featured and quoted in prominent outlets including
[**Wired**](https://www.wired.com/story/google-layoffs-2023-workers/), [**The Verge**](https://www.theverge.com/google/2017/11/20/16681556/apple-swift-language-google-fuchsia-os-open-source), 
[**9to5Google**](https://9to5google.com/2018/04/13/fuchsia-friday-the-dream-team-behind-googles-new-os/), 
and the [**San Francisco Chronicle**](https://www.sfchronicle.com/bayarea/article/Ballot-battle-in-town-of-Alameda-pits-multiunit-15619564.php). 
He has presented at major tech events, including Mobile World Congress, and appeared on international media such as the Dutch TV show [**DWDD University**](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm7838510/).

Zac is also a prolific content creator, with substantial followings on platforms such as
[**TikTok**](https://tiktok.com/@YesInMyBackyard) and [**YouTube**](https://www.youtube.com/@YesInMyBackyard),
where he engages over 140,000 subscribers on issues of urbanism, housing, and policy education.

Zac's broad experience and deep commitment to technology innovation, social advocacy, and community
building mark him as a multifaceted leader shaping both digital and physical landscapes.
